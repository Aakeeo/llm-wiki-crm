# Brightpath Demo Session

## Notes

90-minute demo with Elena Rodriguez (Head of Technology) and James O'Brien (IT Director). Walked through the full LMS workflow — course creation, assignment management, grading, and the student/parent portal.

Key moments:
- Elena loved the teacher dashboard UX — said it was "miles ahead" of their current system
- James asked hard questions about offline mode — showed him the PWA capabilities and local sync. He was satisfied but wants to test in a real low-bandwidth environment
- SSO demo with Google Workspace went smoothly
- Data migration: their current system is a custom PostgreSQL app. James will export a sample dataset for us to do a test migration
- Elena asked about accessibility — confirmed WCAG 2.1 AA compliance

Concerns raised:
- James worried about the small IT team's capacity to manage the transition
- Elena needs ROI materials for the board presentation in May
- Some districts have strict data residency requirements — need on-prem option

Overall positive. Elena said she'll recommend us to the board if the pilot goes well.
