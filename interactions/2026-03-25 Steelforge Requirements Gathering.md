# Steelforge Requirements Gathering

## Notes

60-minute call with Aisha Mohammed (VP Engineering). She walked through their current state:

- 15 plants across Michigan, Ohio, and Indiana
- Mix of old and new equipment — oldest machines from the 1990s, newest from 2023
- Currently using a patchwork of vendor-specific monitoring tools, no centralized view
- Plant managers make decisions based on experience, not data
- They lose ~$2M/year in unplanned downtime across all plants

Her vision:
- Phase 1: Real-time dashboard showing equipment health across all plants
- Phase 2: Predictive maintenance using ML to forecast failures
- Phase 3: Integration with SAP for automated work orders

She wants to start with a single plant pilot at their Detroit facility (50 machines). If successful, roll out to the other 14 plants.

Key constraints:
- Budget isn't allocated yet — she's building the business case for the CIO
- IT team is small and focused on SAP — needs a managed solution
- Factory floor has limited network infrastructure — needs edge computing

Next steps: She'll share equipment inventory and sensor data from the Detroit plant. We'll prepare a pilot proposal with ROI projections.

## Action Items

- Send ROI framework for manufacturing IoT (reduce downtime, improve utilization)
- Prepare pilot proposal for Detroit plant ($50K estimate)
- Research MQTT and OPC-UA integration options for their equipment mix
- Follow up in 2 weeks to check on business case progress
