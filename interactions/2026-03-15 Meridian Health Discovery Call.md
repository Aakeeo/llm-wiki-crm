# Meridian Health Discovery Call

## Notes

45-minute call with Sarah Chen (CTO) and Marcus Johnson (VP Engineering). Sarah outlined their situation: Oracle Health contract expires Q3 2026, they need to migrate 12 hospitals and 200+ clinics to a modern cloud platform. Key requirements:

- HIPAA compliance is non-negotiable
- Phased rollout — can't risk downtime
- Need professional services support for migration

Sarah confirmed they're also talking to Athenahealth but haven't done a demo with them yet. She sees us as the frontrunner based on our conference conversation.
