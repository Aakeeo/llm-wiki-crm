# Steelforge Manufacturing - IoT Dashboard

## Summary

Steelforge wants a centralized IoT dashboard connecting 500+ pieces of factory floor equipment across 15 plants. Phase 1 is real-time monitoring; Phase 2 is predictive maintenance. Very early stage — they're still building the internal business case. Huge deal if it lands.

## Requirements

- Real-time data ingestion from factory floor sensors (MQTT, OPC-UA)
- Dashboard for plant managers: equipment health, utilization, alerts
- Integration with SAP ERP system
- Single plant pilot before full rollout
- Predictive maintenance ML models (Phase 2)
- On-premise edge processing for latency-sensitive data

## Competition

- **Siemens MindSphere** — strong in manufacturing, expensive
- **PTC ThingWorx** — established IoT platform, complex
- **AWS IoT** — generic, would require significant custom development
- Our advantage: faster time to value, better UX, flexible pricing
