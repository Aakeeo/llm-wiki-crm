# Meridian Health - Platform Migration

## Summary

Meridian Health wants to migrate their patient management system from Oracle Health (on-premise) to our cloud platform. 12 hospitals, 200+ clinics. Phased rollout over 6 months. The deal includes platform licensing ($180K/yr), professional services ($40K), and training ($20K).

## Requirements

- HIPAA-compliant cloud infrastructure (SOC 2 Type II required)
- Data migration from Oracle Health with zero data loss
- Phased rollout: 2 pilot hospitals → remaining 10 → clinics
- 99.99% uptime SLA
- 24/7 support during migration
- Integration with their Epic EHR system at 3 facilities
- Sandbox environment for engineering team evaluation

## Competition

- **Athenahealth** — in early conversations but haven't done a demo yet
- Our advantage: modern cloud architecture, better API, lower total cost of ownership
