# Brightpath Education - LMS Rollout

## Summary

Brightpath wants to replace their custom-built LMS with our platform across 200+ school districts. The deal includes platform licensing ($120K/yr), data migration ($20K), and training ($10K). Nonprofit procurement — requires board approval.

## Requirements

- Offline-capable for rural districts with limited internet
- SSO integration with Google Workspace and Microsoft 365
- Data migration from custom-built LMS (PostgreSQL backend)
- On-premise deployment option for districts with data residency requirements
- Accessibility compliance (WCAG 2.1 AA)
- Teacher training program and documentation

## Competition

- **Canvas (Instructure)** — strong brand in education, higher price point
- **Schoology (PowerSchool)** — already used by some districts, switching cost is low
- Our advantage: offline support, lower cost for nonprofits, modern UX
