# RouteAI - Logistics Analytics

## Summary

RouteAI wants to embed a self-serve analytics dashboard into their route optimization platform. Their 200 delivery company customers need delivery performance metrics — on-time rates, driver efficiency, cost-per-delivery trends. Currently handled by manual SQL queries. Estimated ~$80K ARR based on 15M events/month at standard usage-based tiers.

## Requirements

- Embeddable analytics dashboard (white-labeled, iframe)
- Real-time data: delivery status, driver location, ETA accuracy
- Historical trends: on-time %, cost per delivery, volume by region
- Multi-tenant: each delivery company sees only their own data
- API access for customers with their own BI tools
- Usage-based pricing tied to delivery volume (~500K deliveries/day)

## Competition

- **Build in-house** — they have a data team but it's small and focused on route optimization ML
- Unknown if evaluating other analytics vendors
